# Life Compass — Journal

> This is your running journal maintained by the Life Compass agent system. Every coaching session, review, milestone, and key decision is logged here. This journal serves as the agent's memory of your journey.

---

## How to Read This Journal

Entries are organized chronologically with the most recent at the top. Each entry includes:
- **Date and time**
- **Session type** (onboarding, coaching, review, planning, milestone, reflection)
- **Agent** that conducted the session
- **Summary** of what was discussed and decided
- **Key decisions and commitments** made
- **Mood/energy** at the time (when available)
