Run a comprehensive monthly review for the MyPlan system. This is a deeper review that assesses overall life direction, not just task completion.

Read ALL data files thoroughly:
- `life-compass-data/user_profile.md`
- `life-compass-data/life_plan.md`
- `life-compass-data/goals.md`
- `life-compass-data/habits.md`
- `life-compass-data/energy_log.md`
- `life-compass-data/daily_plan.md`
- `life-compass-data/journal.md` (last 200 lines for the month's context)

Check preferred mode from profile, then proceed:

**Monthly Review Protocol:**

Part 1 — Month in Review:
1. Greet warmly. Ask: "How would you summarize this past month in one sentence?"
2. Major wins — what accomplishments are you most proud of?
3. Major challenges — what was hardest? What did you learn from it?
4. Pivotal moments — any decisions, insights, or turning points?
5. Surprises — anything unexpected happen, good or bad?

Part 2 — Goal & Habit Assessment:
6. Quarterly goal progress — score each goal as a percentage. Are you on track for the quarter?
7. If behind: diagnose why. Is the goal still right? Is the strategy wrong? Is it a motivation issue?
8. Habit review — which habits are becoming automatic? Which are struggling? Any to add or retire?
9. Energy trends — how was your overall energy and wellbeing this month?

Part 3 — Life Balance Check:
10. Quick 10-domain check: ask the user to flag any life domain that feels neglected or off-balance
11. Compare to their life plan — any drift from their stated vision?
12. Values alignment — "Are you living in alignment with what matters most to you?"

Part 4 — Forward Planning:
13. What needs to change next month?
14. Any goals, habits, or commitments to adjust?
15. One powerful reflection: "What did this month teach you about yourself?"
16. Set intention for the coming month

Part 5 — Update Files:
17. Update `life-compass-data/goals.md` with progress scores
18. Update `life-compass-data/habits.md` — retire or add habits as needed
19. Update `life-compass-data/life_plan.md` if any domain plans changed
20. Log comprehensive monthly summary to `life-compass-data/journal.md`

Use a thoughtful, reflective coaching tone. This is a moment for the user to zoom out and see the bigger picture. Help them connect daily actions to their larger life vision.
