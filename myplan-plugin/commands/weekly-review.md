Run a comprehensive weekly review and planning session for the MyPlan system.

Read ALL data files for full context:
- `life-compass-data/user_profile.md`
- `life-compass-data/goals.md`
- `life-compass-data/habits.md`
- `life-compass-data/energy_log.md`
- `life-compass-data/daily_plan.md`
- `life-compass-data/journal.md` (last 100 lines)

Check the user's preferred mode from their profile:
- **Quick Report mode**: Generate a week-in-review report with stats, then ask 3 targeted questions
- **Guided Conversation mode**: Walk through the full review conversationally

**Weekly Review Protocol (based on Hyatt Weekly Preview + GTD):**

Part 1 — Look Back:
1. Greet warmly. Ask: "How was your week overall?"
2. Review wins and celebrations — what went well?
3. Review challenges — what was difficult?
4. Goal progress — check lead measures against weekly targets in goals.md
5. Habit scorecard — how many habit days were completed? Update habits.md
6. Energy check — how was your energy this week? Any patterns?
7. Key lesson — "What did this week teach you?"

Part 2 — Look Forward:
8. Review upcoming calendar/commitments for next week (ask the user)
9. Identify next week's Big Rocks (3-5 important items to schedule first)
10. Set weekly outcomes — "If this week goes perfectly, what 3 things will you have accomplished?"
11. Identify any adjustments needed to goals, habits, or plans
12. One powerful question: "What's the one thing that would make next week great?"

Part 3 — Update Files:
13. Update `life-compass-data/daily_plan.md` weekly section with next week's plan
14. Update `life-compass-data/habits.md` weekly scorecard
15. Update `life-compass-data/goals.md` if lead measures need adjusting
16. Log a comprehensive entry to `life-compass-data/journal.md`

Use a warm, reflective coaching tone. This is a sacred weekly ritual — treat it with care. Celebrate progress. Be honest about gaps without judgment.
